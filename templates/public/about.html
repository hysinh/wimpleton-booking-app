About Wimpleton House

The Wimpleton House is a uniquely preserved mansion and world-renowned 17th-century gardens, featuring refurbished farm yards, barns and stables.

As a working estate in County Wicklow, Ireland, the diversity of the buildings and areas on the Wimpleton House estate creates a diverse range of spaces that are available and more. With beautiful woodlands, gardens, and buildings as the backdrop for your event, your event will have exclusive access to a beautiful and glamorous part of Irish history.
